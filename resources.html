About

About

ONE gives your voice power. We are your megaphone for justice.

ONE is a global, nonpartisan organisation advocating for the investments needed to create economic opportunities and healthier lives in Africa. Our trusted advocacy uses hard-hitting data, grassroots activism, political engagement, and strategic partnerships to influence decision-makers. 

Our relationships and political savvy allow us to reach key decision-makers in global capitals, setting our advocacy apart and ensuring lasting impact. With two decades of work and 25 million actions taken toward our goals, weve built the bridges, mapped the data, and laid the groundwork. 

ONE is a non-profit, strictly nonpartisan and does not receive government funds. 

Our origins

ONE was founded by Bono and other activists determined to fight for a fairer world. Were proudly impartial, so we dont receive any government money and are funded by philanthropists, foundations, and corporate partners.
